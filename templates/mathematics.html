📖 History of Mathematics

Mathematics has been studied for thousands of years. Ancient civilizations like the Egyptians and Babylonians developed early techniques for counting, measuring, and solving equations. Greek mathematicians such as Euclid and Pythagoras laid the foundations of geometry and number theory. In modern times, mathematics continues to evolve with applications in technology, AI, and physics.

👨‍🏫 Famous Mathematicians

  • Euclid – Father of Geometry
  • Isaac Newton – Invented Calculus
  • Srinivasa Ramanujan – Great Indian Mathematician
  • Carl Gauss – Prince of Mathematicians
